# Cau hoi thuong gap (FAQ)

## Lam sao de dat hang?
Dang nhap tai khoan, chon sach muon mua, them vao gio hang. Sau do vao gio hang de dat hang, chon dia chi giao hang va phuong thuc thanh toan.

## Toi co the huy don hang khong?
Don hang chi co the huy khi o trang thai PENDING hoac CONFIRMED. Sau khi da thanh toan (PAID) hoac dang giao (SHIPPING), khong the huy don hang.

## Lam sao de theo doi don hang?
Dang nhap va vao muc "My Orders" tren thanh menu. Ban se thay trang thai don hang va ma van don (neu da giao).

## Toi quen mat khau, lam sao?
Hien tai he thong chua ho tro quen mat khau tu dong. Vui long lien he bo phan ho tro de duoc ho tro.

## Sach co duoc boc ni long khong?
Tat ca sach deu duoc boc ni long va dong goi can than truoc khi giao hang.

## Co giam gia cho don hang lon khong?
Hien tai BookStore chua co chuong trinh giam gia cho don hang lon. Tuy nhien, don hang tren 300.000 VND duoc mien phi giao hang tieu chuan.

## Toi muon goi y sach, lam sao?
Ban co the su dung tinh nang "For You" (Goi y cho ban) tren thanh menu de xem sach duoc goi y dua tren danh gia cong dong. Ngoai ra, ban co the hoi tu van vien AI qua chat widget.

## Co the danh gia sach khong?
Co! Sau khi mua sach, ban co the vao trang chi tiet sach va de lai danh gia (1-5 sao) va binh luan.

## BookStore co ung dung mobile khong?
Hien tai BookStore chua co ung dung mobile. Ban co the truy cap website tren trinh duyet dien thoai.
