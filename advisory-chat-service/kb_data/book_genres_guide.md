# Huong dan the loai sach tai BookStore

## Van hoc Viet Nam
Bao gom tieu thuyet, truyen ngan, tho ca cua cac tac gia Viet Nam noi tieng.
Phu hop cho nguoi yeu thich van hoc truyen thong, tim hieu van hoa Viet Nam.
Cac tac pham tieu bieu: Truyen Kieu (Nguyen Du), Tat Den (Ngo Tat To), Chi Pheo (Nam Cao).
Gia tham khao: 55.000 - 85.000 VND.

## Khoa hoc & Cong nghe
Sach ve lap trinh, khoa hoc may tinh, cong nghe thong tin, khoa hoc tu nhien.
Phu hop cho sinh vien CNTT, lap trinh vien, nguoi yeu thich cong nghe.
Cac tac pham tieu bieu: Clean Code (Robert C. Martin), Python Crash Course (Eric Matthes), Design Patterns (Gang of Four).
Gia tham khao: 350.000 - 480.000 VND.

## Kinh te & Kinh doanh
Sach ve tai chinh ca nhan, quan tri kinh doanh, dau tu, khoi nghiep.
Phu hop cho doanh nhan, nha quan ly, nguoi muon hoc ve tai chinh.
Cac tac pham tieu bieu: Cha Giau Cha Ngheo (Robert Kiyosaki), Nghi Giau Lam Giau (Napoleon Hill).
Gia tham khao: 95.000 - 110.000 VND.

## Thieu nhi
Truyen tranh, sach giao duc, sach interactive cho tre em tu 3-15 tuoi.
Phu hop lam qua tang, phat trien tri tuong tuong va kha nang doc cho tre.
Cac tac pham tieu bieu: Doraemon (Fujiko F. Fujio), Conan (Gosho Aoyama), Cho Toi Xin Mot Ve Di Tuoi Tho (Nguyen Nhat Anh).
Gia tham khao: 25.000 - 68.000 VND.

## Ky nang song
Sach phat trien ban than, tam ly hoc, ky nang giao tiep, quan ly thoi gian.
Phu hop cho nguoi muon cai thien ban than, xay dung thoi quen tot.
Cac tac pham tieu bieu: Nha Gia Kim (Paulo Coelho), Dac Nhan Tam (Dale Carnegie), Tuoi Tre Dang Gia Bao Nhieu (Rosie Nguyen).
Gia tham khao: 75.000 - 88.000 VND.
